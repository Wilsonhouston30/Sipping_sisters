Overview

Purpose

Sippin' Sisters, a forthcoming startup set to debut in February 2024, caters to a vibrant demographic encompassing college students and individuals fond of non-alcoholic beverages. Despite an initial expectation of minimal website traffic, the team is committed to crafting an enticing online platform. With a keen eye on aesthetics and functionality, the goal is to ensure that even with limited traffic, the website captivates visitors with its appeal, potentially sparking interest and engagement among the target audience.

Audience

Commercial Businesses and college students
